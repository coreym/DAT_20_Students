#Name of Algorithm
Support Vector Machines

#Problem it solves
Predicting  specific outcome such as response or no-response

#How it works
SVM works by finding the best seperating line or hyperplane between points that are closest to the outcome boundaries

#Applications in business
-Spam mail filtering
-Classifying an individual as high or low risk
-Determining if a customer will buy a product or use a service again
